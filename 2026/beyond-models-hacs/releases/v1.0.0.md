**Release Date:** 2026-07-23

## Overview

This release introduces the first public research framework for **Human–AI Collaborative Systems (HACS)**.

The framework explores a transition from model-centric artificial intelligence toward systems designed for long-term human–AI collaboration.

## Research Focus

This release explores:

- Collaborative Intelligence
- Context Continuity
- Human Agency Preservation
- Trust Calibration
- Responsible AI Governance
- Long-term Human–AI Interaction

---

## Research Position

This framework does not claim that current AI systems possess consciousness or human intention.

It proposes a research direction for studying how humans and AI systems can collaborate effectively while preserving human responsibility.
