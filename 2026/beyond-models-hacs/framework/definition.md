# Human–AI Collaborative Systems (HACS)

## Definition

A Human–AI Collaborative System (HACS) is a socio-technical system in which human expertise and artificial intelligence capabilities interact through persistent context, governance mechanisms, and iterative feedback processes to improve outcomes over time.

## Purpose

HACS studies collaboration as the primary unit of analysis, moving beyond evaluation of isolated model performance toward evaluation of long-term human–AI cooperation.

## Core Principles

- Human responsibility remains central.
- AI capability is a collaborative resource.
- Context continuity enables long-term interaction.
- Governance provides responsible operation.
- Collaboration quality requires continuous evaluation.
