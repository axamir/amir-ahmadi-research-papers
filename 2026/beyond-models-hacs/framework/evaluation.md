# HACS Evaluation Framework

## Proposed Metrics

### Context Continuity Score (CCS)
Measures preservation of meaningful context across interactions.

### Trust Calibration Index (TCI)
Measures whether human trust matches actual AI capability.

### Human Agency Preservation Score (HAPS)
Measures whether AI collaboration strengthens human decision-making.

### Alignment Stability Score (ASS)
Measures stability of collaborative goals over time.

### Recovery Capability Score (RCS)
Measures the ability to recognize and recover from errors.
