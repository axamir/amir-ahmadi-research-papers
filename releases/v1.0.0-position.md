# v1.0.0 Position Statement

## Beyond Models: Toward Enduring Human–AI Collaborative Systems

Version: v1.0.0
Status: Initial Research Release

## Purpose

Version 1.0.0 represents the first stable research snapshot of the Human–AI Collaborative System (HACS) framework.

It is not presented as a final theory. It establishes the initial vocabulary, principles, and research direction for studying long-term human–AI collaboration.

## What v1.0.0 Establishes

- Human–AI Collaborative Systems (HACS) as a socio-technical research framework
- A shift from model capability alone toward collaboration quality
- Context Continuity as a research concept beyond simple memory
- Collaborative Intelligence as an outcome of human and AI interaction
- Governance and human agency as essential design principles

## What v1.0.0 Does Not Claim

- Artificial consciousness
- Human replacement by AI
- Final solutions for AI collaboration
- Empirical validation of all proposed concepts

## Research Evolution

Future versions will expand this foundation through:

- literature positioning
- domain-informed contributions
- case studies
- empirical evaluation
- critique and refinement

The v1.0.0 release remains the historical reference point for the evolution of HACS.
