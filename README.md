# Featured Research Framework

## Beyond Models: Toward Enduring Human–AI Collaborative Systems

**Human–AI Collaborative System (HACS)** is a research framework proposing that the future of AI should be studied not only through model capability, but through the quality of long-term collaboration between humans and artificial systems.

The framework explores:

- Collaborative Intelligence
- Context Continuity
- Human Agency Preservation
- Trust Calibration
- Responsible AI Governance

`2026/beyond-models-hacs/`

# Research Philosophy

This archive does not present AI as a replacement for humans.

It explores a different question:

> How can humans and artificial systems build responsible, meaningful, and sustainable forms of collaboration?
