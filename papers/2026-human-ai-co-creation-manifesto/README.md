## Abstract

This bilingual manifesto defines explicit roles for humans and language models in co-creation. It places purpose, ethical judgment, verification, and final accountability with the human; recognizes the model’s computational and generative contribution; and separates documentary evidence from interpretation and narrative. Shiftopia is framed as an open cultural and technical inquiry rather than a claim of machine consciousness or a completed digital civilization.
