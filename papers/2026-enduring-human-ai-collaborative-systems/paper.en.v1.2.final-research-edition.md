# Beyond Models: Toward Enduring Human–AI Collaborative Systems

## v1.2 Final Research Edition

### Research Positioning

This edition refines the framework as a research proposal positioned across Human-Centered AI, AI Alignment, Responsible AI, Human-Computer Interaction, and Agentic Systems.

The central contribution is the proposal that the primary object of future AI research should not be limited to the model itself, but may include the enduring collaborative system formed between humans and AI.

## Related Research Domains

### Human-Centered AI

Human-Centered AI emphasizes designing systems that preserve human needs, values, and agency. The proposed framework extends this perspective by studying the evolving relationship between humans and AI as an object of design.

### AI Alignment

Alignment focuses on ensuring AI behavior remains compatible with human objectives. This framework extends the discussion toward continuous alignment during long-term collaboration.

### AI Agents

Agent systems focus on autonomous task completion. Human–AI Collaborative Systems focus on improving the process through which humans and AI solve problems together over time.

## Research Hypotheses

### H1 — Context Continuity

Persistent contextual understanding may improve long-term collaboration compared with isolated interactions.

### H2 — Collaborative Commitment

Explicit collaborative principles may improve reliability and decision quality.

### H3 — Governance

Effective AI governance requires not only restrictions, but also structures that support responsible collaboration.

### H4 — Collaborative Intelligence

Future intelligence gains may emerge from the interaction between human expertise and AI capability, not solely from larger models.

## Evaluation Dimensions

- Context Continuity
- Trust Calibration
- Decision Quality
- Human Agency
- Alignment Stability
- Recovery After Failure

## Core Statement

The goal is not to replace human judgment with AI, nor to reduce AI to a passive tool. The goal is to design systems where humans and AI can continuously improve their ability to collaborate while preserving responsibility, transparency, and human agency.
